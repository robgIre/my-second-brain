Get Started
Three steps to connect your Second Brain. Takes about 2 minutes.
1

Open your OD terminal

Connect to your On-Demand server using dev connect from your laptop terminal.

2

Paste the setup command

Copy this command and paste it into your terminal. It installs everything and starts your brain.

git clone https://github.com/robgIre/my-second-brain.git && cd my-second-brain && bash setup.sh
3

Click "Connect Brain" on the home page

Once the server is running, come back here and click the green Connect Brain button on the Home page.

After setup

Go to About Me and fill in your details — this teaches your brain who you are. Then try running your Morning Routine, or type anything into Ask My Brain.

💡
Troubleshooting: If "Connect Brain" fails, check that Claude Code is installed on your OD by running claude --version in your terminal. Questions? Message Rob Goldsmith on GChat.

How It Works
Everything you need to know about your Second Brain.

🧠 What is the Second Brain?

An AI-powered personal assistant that remembers everything about you, your projects, and your work. It uses Claude Code with PARA (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) to organise your knowledge and automate routine tasks. Think of it as a chief of staff that never forgets.

💬 What is Ask My Brain?

Type or speak any task in plain language. Your brain translates it into actions and runs them. You see progress in real-time. No commands, no terminal, no code.

🗄️ How does it remember?

Your brain stores information in a file called CLAUDE.md. The About Me page and routines auto-sync to this file. Every new session reads it first so it knows who you are and what you're working on.

⚡ What are Routines?

Sets of instructions in plain language. Click "Run" and your brain executes each step. Morning Routine can review your calendar, check tasks, and prioritize your day — all in about 3 minutes.

🔒 Is my data safe?

Yes. Everything runs within Meta's infrastructure. Your data stays on your server and syncs via Google Drive. Nothing leaves Meta's network.
